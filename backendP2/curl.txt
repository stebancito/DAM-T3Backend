## Probar conexión
curl http://localhost:5000

## Post de registro
curl -X POST http://localhost:5000/register -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d '{"username":"juan","password":"1234"}'

## Post de login
curl -X POST http://localhost:5000/login -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d '{"username":"juan","password":"1234"}'
